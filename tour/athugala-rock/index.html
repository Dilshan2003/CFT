The Athugala Rock Of Kurunegala, Also Known As The Rathugala Rock, Is An Iconic Rock Formation Located In The Sri Lankan City Of Kurunegala. The Rock Was First Used As A Lookout Point By King Bhuvanekabahu In 1287 And Has Been Heavily Photographed Ever Since Its Discovery. In Recent Years, It Has Been Listed As One Of The Top 10 Places To Visit In Sri Lanka, Both For Its Aesthetic Value And Historical Significance.
